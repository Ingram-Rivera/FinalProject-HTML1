Welcome to the Malware Information Website

This website is a useful tool if you want to know about all types of malware and what damage they can cause.

What is malware?

Malware is a shortened term for "Malicious Software". Malware is an application or piece of code that causes harm or unwanted effects on a computer device. Some types of malware include viruses, trojans, worms, and rootkits. Click the malware list above for more information.

How can I protect my devices from malware?

One of the main ways to protect your computer is to stay on safe websites. Lesser known sites can be unsafe and may contain code that executes malware in it. Another way to prevent malware is to check what you are installing with an anti-malware application. The VirusTotal website is trusted by many individuals and runs any file you upload through multiple anti-malware programs.

How do I use this website?

You can either click the Random Malware link in the navigation bar, or click on Malware List to choose a specific topic. You can also click on the Contact Us link if you want more information or have something to share with us.
